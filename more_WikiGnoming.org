A couple of people have complained to me that they have trouble
"finding their way back" to pages that interest them on AsteroidMeta.
We can't do a whole lot to improve the medium, but we can improve the
quality of the content.  So: organize pages, and (especially!) add categories
to pages that need them.

For one related technological innovation, check out [http://www.oddmuse.org/cgi-bin/oddmuse/Subscribed%20Recent%20Changes this oddmuse extension].
