 1. If /(x y) elt (A times B)/ then
/(x y) elt A times (B union C)/

 1. If /(x y) elt (A times C)/ then
/(x y) elt A times (B union C)/

 1. /(A times B) union (A times C) subset A times (B union C)/

 1. /A times (B union C) = (A times B) union (A times C)/
